# --------------------------- Gameplay -------------------------#
| We have three choices include Rock, Paper and Scissor.        |
| Follow these rules below:                                     |
|    + Rock win scissor                                         |
|    + Scissor win paper                                        |
|    + Paper win rock.                                          |
| We will play 3 rounds and who win 2 rounds will win the game. |
